About Rookie

Gordon "Rookie" Reardon was one of the influential figures in New Zealand basketball during the 1970s, (At that time) he was the youngest person ever selected into New Zealands National Basketball Team at 17 years old.

That led to him becoming known as "Rookie" a nickname that stayed with him throughout his entire basketball career and one by which he is still known today. A legendary achievement in itself.

Featured Archive

USA Big Ten All-Stars vs New Zealand

On 29 June 1971 an American collegiate team known as the Big Ten All-Stars played an exhibition basketball game against the New Zealand Team at Tauranga’s Queen Elizabeth Youth Centre (QEYC). Rookie Reardon playing in the white NZ number 12 singlet. The U.S. All-Stars won by a large margin, 107–73.
